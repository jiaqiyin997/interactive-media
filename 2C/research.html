Simple Arduino-Controlled, No-Pump Plant Watering. I love this simple and clever design. It basically uses a microcontroller-powered servo motor to pinch a watering hose on/off on a gravity-fed plant watering system. No pump required. It makes me think about the combination of ecology and interactive technology.
Arduino Powered Headdress. This project recreates Moritz Waldemeyer’s
Automaton headdress using 10 mm EVA foam, 5 mm foamboard,
15 servos, 85 RGB LEDs, two Arduino Pro Minis, and a backpack
control box. It is quite beautiful. It makes me think about aesthetic–technical Synthesis blending compelling visuals with underlying technology.
