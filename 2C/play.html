Temperature-controlled ice beer handle
When the beer is too hot, the handle
automatically starts the cooling chip to cool it down.
Arduino reads the temperature sensor, and automatically
powers the semiconductor cooling chip when the temperature
is high.
Automatic shoe-removing and slipper-removing machine
When you get home tired, take off your shoes and put on
slippers at the same time. Arduino receives the Bluetooth key signal,
the motor drives the shoe sole to pop open, and the slippers automatically come on the
feet.
Mopping hat
Mopping the floor while walking, doing housework
at the same time. How to use: hang a mini mop
strip under the brim of the hat, and mop the floor
while walking.
